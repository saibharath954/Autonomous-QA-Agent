# Product Specifications: E-Shop Checkout

## Discount Codes
1. **SAVE15**: Applies a flat 15% discount to the subtotal.
2. **FREESHIP**: Waives the shipping fee.
3. **Invalid Codes**: Should display "Invalid Code" next to the input field.

## Shipping Rules
1. **Standard Shipping**: Free of charge. Delivery in 5-7 business days.
2. **Express Shipping**: Flat rate of $10. Delivery in 1-2 business days.

## Cart Logic
1. Minimum quantity for any item is 1.
2. "Update" button recalculates the subtotal based on current quantities.
